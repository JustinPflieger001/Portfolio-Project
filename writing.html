Many people have stood up in the fight against cyberbullying, to help those in need and to prevent further harm. After their daughter killed herself due to cyberbullying, the parents of Megan Meier formed a foundation to raise awareness and to help people under attack by cyberbullies. “stopbullying.gov” is a government website dedicated to spreading information to the masses about topics such as: what is bullying, the roles kids play, who is at risk, warning signs of bullying, effects of bullying, what is cyberbullying, cyberbullying tactics, prevent cyberbullying, digital awareness for parents, and so much more. The Cyberbullying Research Center is an educational website that tells about the laws. research, resources, and much more that is involved with cyberbullying. KidsHealth tells of the many dangers in a child's life including bullying and cyberbullying.STOP cyberbullying tells of the current epidemic of cyberbullying, its topics include: what is cyberbullying, how does it work, why cyberbully, prevention, take action, what’s the law, stop cyberbullying toolkit, and more.

Todesrand is a multi-continental planet, and is called home by many people. D’aahrc is the Main continent, and holds many races within its borders. In the center is a walled citadel, known as the Bastion, where the High Elves reside. Surrounding the Bastion is another city, Derrick, which is open to all races. In the snowy north west lies The Market, a sanctuary town filled with stores, pubs, and inns of all types. To the right of The Market is the Enchanted Forest, a place of magical creatures lit by the bioluminescence of the mushroom trees. West of the Bastion is the woodlands, home of the wood elves, forest gnomes, and other creatures of the forest. In the south lies the Volcano, called The Ember Mountain, surrounding is a spiked mountain wall, and within these walls lie the Orcs. Right of the orcish land is the Oblivion Summit, a mountain reaching heights of 29,029ft. The bottom of the mountain holds The Smiths of the Lost Age, a clan of Gnomes and Dwarves dedicated to technological advancements, in their hold, The Forge. Above the Smiths are the AArakocra, living atop the tall mountain trees in the city called The Nest. Above the Bird-folk, at the mountain’s peak, lies the Goliath’s city of Elen Vu, a place few outsiders have seen. South of the summit, are the Merfolkian Isles, home to Tortles, Tritons, and other sea creatures. West of the Bastion is the Fin Brod Golt, home of the Dragonborn clans. In the clouds lies the city of the Aasimar, commonly called The Heavens.

Todesrand is a world plagued by Corruption, death, and evil. The main continent, known simply as D’aahrc, is fueled by hatred and fear. The High Elven race had taken power in the Battle of King’s Betrayal, and has been ruling the land with an iron fist, creating the Malignant Era. The Battle of King’s Betrayal took place in the Regression Age(200 years ago), and lasted 50 years. The war devastated the nation's infrastructure,caused widespread famines, and one out of every four people were unemployed. The High Elves used their riches to buy their power and fixed parts of the country. An elven Mage raised mountains and sealed the Orcs in around The Ember Mountain.

The human race is one of the top species of the food chain and historically the smartest race known, but if anything, the human race is inventive. Since the dawn of Man, humans have invented, from fire, to wheel, to telephones, humans have invented, and are now attempting to create a counterfeit life. The look of an AI is often debated, Hiroshi Ishiguro, a Japanese professor in Osaka University's Department of Adaptive Machine Systems, believes “robots... look, act... feel... humans”.Japanese culture is very robot-centric, they are used as toys from early childhood, there are special places for androids in Japan, and they are constantly shown in cartoons and movies which makes Japanese people much more accepting of automatons. Stephen Keeney, the project leader for Honda America's North American Advanced Step in Innovative Mobility (ASIMO), says “they [Artificial Intelligences]... look... artificial… humans… accept...”. While Japan has commercialized androids as good and helpful, the United States of America(USA) has created many movies depicting robots disguised as humans leading to the end of the world. Eliezer Yudkowsky, a researcher at the Singularity Institute for Artificial Intelligence, argues “... responsibility… create... not merely... intelligence, but… humaneness, needing… altruism… love... beauty”. Yudkowsky explains how without an emotional capability, the Intelligence would not care about the human race and could exterminate it without any emotion or regret.
